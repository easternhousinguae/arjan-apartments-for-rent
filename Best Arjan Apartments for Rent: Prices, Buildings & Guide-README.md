Transportation & Connectivity

Arjan is strategically located near Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Road and Umm Suqeim Road, giving residents quick access to the rest of the city. While the community does not have a direct metro station yet, frequent RTA buses connect Arjan to nearby areas like Mall of the Emirates.

For drivers, reaching key parts of Dubai is easy:

-   Mall of the Emirates: 10–12 minutes
    
-   Dubai Marina: 15–18 minutes
    
-   Downtown Dubai: 20 minutes
    
-   Dubai Airport: 25–28 minutes
